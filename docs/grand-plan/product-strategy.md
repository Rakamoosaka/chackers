# Product Strategy

## Problem

Most checkers websites are outdated, isolated, and do not create a reason to return. They usually provide a board, but they rarely provide progression, social context, learning, or a modern competitive feeling.

## Solution

Chackers turns checkers into a fast competitive learning platform with AI coaching, daily puzzles, ranked progression, and social discovery.

## Differentiation

### Competitive Identity

The player should feel like they are entering a ranked platform, not opening a toy board.

Core elements:

- Elo-style rating.
- League badges.
- Fast match formats.
- Friend-link multiplayer as a visible product direction.
- Match history.
- Player profile.
- Win rate and streaks.

### Learning Loop

The app should help users improve after each game.

Core elements:

- AI Coach summary.
- Missed capture detection.
- Risky move detection.
- Accuracy estimate.
- Daily tactical puzzles.

### Retention Loop

The app should give users a reason to come back.

Core elements:

- Puzzle streak.
- Match history.
- Rating progression.
- City leaderboard.
- Unlockable skins.

### Social Loop

The app should feel bigger than a private board.

Core elements:

- City leaderboard.
- Global leaderboard by city.
- Watch Party prototype.
- Friend-link multiplayer prototype.
- Simulated live matches.
- Emoji reactions.
- Future friend link multiplayer.

## Business Model

### Free Tier

- Basic play.
- AI opponent.
- Backend-saved match history.
- Daily puzzle.
- Limited AI Coach analysis.
- Basic board skins.

### Pro Tier

- Unlimited AI Coach.
- Advanced game analytics.
- Deeper move explanations.
- Premium board and piece skins.
- Future tournament participation.

### Cosmetic Purchases

Potential one-time purchases:

- Neon board.
- Minimal board.
- Kazakh ornament inspired board.
- Premium piece sets.

### Future Revenue

- Sponsored city leagues.
- School and university competitions.
- Tournament entry fees.
- Premium training packs.

## Product Metrics

Track these in a real product:

- DAU/MAU.
- Puzzle streak retention.
- Average games per user.
- Rematch rate.
- AI Coach usage.
- Conversion to Pro.
- Churn after first session.
- Share/invite rate.

## Roadmap

### Next

- Production hardening for Supabase auth and profile persistence.
- Real multiplayer by invite link using Supabase Realtime.
- Online ranked matchmaking.

### Later

- Live tournaments.
- City clan battles.
- University leagues.
- AI personalities.
- Real Stripe checkout.
- User-generated puzzle packs.

## Positioning Statement

Chackers is a competitive, mobile-first checkers platform that combines fast matches, AI coaching, daily training, and social progression to make checkers feel modern and replayable.
